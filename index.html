About Us

Rink D&S is a food technology startup that provides consulting, data vendor, and lab information management services to help create new and innovative food products. Our team of food technology experts leverage molecular and market data to optimize the nutritional value, sustainability, functionality, flavor, and price of food products.

Our software simplifies and streamlines the food product development process, making it accessible to anyone with a passion for food and a desire to share it with others. Whether you're a food manufacturer looking to improve your products or a consumer interested in healthier food options, Rink D&S has the expertise and technology to help.
